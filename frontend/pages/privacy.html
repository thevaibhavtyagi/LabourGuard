Overview

LabourGuard is designed from the ground up to be a privacy-first labour law compliance system. We believe that ensuring workplace compliance should not require invasive surveillance or monitoring of employees.

Our approach is simple: collect only the essential data needed to track working hours and ensure legal compliance — nothing more. This means no location tracking, no screen monitoring, no keystroke logging, and no background surveillance of any kind.

This privacy policy explains in clear, plain language exactly what data we collect, why we collect it, how we protect it, and what rights you have over your information.

What We Collect

We collect only the minimum data necessary to provide our service. Here is a complete and exhaustive list of all data we store:

Name

Your first and last name for identification within the system.

Email Address

Used for authentication and essential account communications.

Password (Hashed)

Securely hashed using bcrypt. We never store plain-text passwords.

Work Timestamps

Check-in and check-out times you explicitly record.

Computed Hours

Total working hours calculated from your timestamps.

Compliance Violations

Records when labour law limits are exceeded for legal compliance.

That's it. The above list is complete and exhaustive. We do not collect any other data about you, your device, your location, or your activities.

What We Don't Collect

Unlike many workplace monitoring tools, LabourGuard explicitly refuses to collect invasive data. Here is what we will never collect:

Location Data

No GPS, IP-based location, or geofencing. We never track where you are.

Screen Captures

No screenshots, screen recordings, or visual monitoring of any kind.

Keystroke Logging

No recording of what you type, including passwords or personal messages.

Application Usage

No tracking of which apps or websites you use during work.

Mouse/Activity Tracking

No monitoring of mouse movements, clicks, or "activity levels."

Webcam/Microphone

No access to camera or microphone. No selfie verification or audio recording.

Background Processes

No hidden agents, daemons, or background processes running on your device.

File Access

No scanning of files, documents, or personal data on your device.

Why Privacy-First

Many workplace monitoring tools justify invasive surveillance in the name of productivity or compliance. We fundamentally disagree with this approach.

Compliance ≠ Surveillance

Labour law compliance only requires tracking hours worked, not monitoring every action.

Trust Builds Productivity

Surveillance erodes trust. Privacy-respecting tools foster better work environments.

Employee Rights Matter

Workers deserve dignity and privacy. Our tool respects fundamental rights.

Less Data = Less Risk

We can't leak or misuse data we never collect in the first place.

How Data Is Used

The data we collect is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Authentication: Your email and hashed password verify your identity when logging in.
  • Time Tracking: Work timestamps are used to calculate your daily and weekly hours.
  • Compliance Monitoring: Working hours are compared against legal limits (e.g., 9 hours/day, 48 hours/week) to detect violations.
  • Employer Reports: Aggregated, anonymized hours data helps employers ensure organization-wide compliance.
  • Violation Records: When limits are exceeded, violations are recorded for legal compliance documentation.

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties for advertising, marketing, or any other purpose. Your data stays within LabourGuard.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Password Hashing: All passwords are hashed using bcrypt with a cost factor of 12, making them computationally infeasible to reverse.
  • HTTPS Encryption: All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using TLS.
  • JWT Authentication: Secure, stateless authentication tokens with expiration prevent unauthorized access.
  • Database Security: MongoDB instances are configured with authentication and access controls.
  • Input Validation: All user inputs are validated and sanitized to prevent injection attacks.

Your Rights

You have full control over your data. Your rights include:

  • Right to Access: You can view all data we have about you at any time through your dashboard.
  • Right to Rectification: You can update your personal information (name, email) in your account settings.
  • Right to Deletion: You can request complete deletion of your account and all associated data.
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request an export of your data in a standard format.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: You can stop using our service at any time without penalty.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the information provided below.
